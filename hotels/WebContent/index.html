The Royal castle hotel is an original, contemporary, boutique hotel in India, providing a point of calm and inspiration in the urban hub. This centrally located hotel offers inspiring design, refreshing people, rejuvenating spaces, innovative dining, sensuous entertainment, instant connectivity and edgy art, balancing mind and body, work and play.

It is the finest 4 star luxury hotel in Chennai and is widely known for its vibrant and thematic restaurants and for being a haven of comfort and style. Set in acres of lush, landscaped gardens, the tri-building property is designed for both leisure and business travelers who seek a superior degree of comfort, hospitality and personalized service.

Our passion, care and imagination are constantly reflected in our effort to uphold the highest standards of service and hospitality. Our associates never fail to raise the bar as they deliver on the promise of unfading excellence, setting the benchmark for quality of service on the global hospitality stage.
